BMW M3 Manual

The BMW M3 Manual is a rare gem in the world of performance sedans. Known for its precision engineering and exhilarating driving dynamics, the M3 stands as a testament to BMW’s commitment to “The Ultimate Driving Machine.”

Equipped with a 6-speed manual transmission, the M3 delivers an engaging experience for driving enthusiasts. The naturally aspirated inline-6 or turbocharged engines provide ample power, while the manual gearbox allows for full control, making every shift a rewarding experience.

Whether you're on the track or cruising down the highway, the BMW M3 Manual offers unparalleled connection between driver and machine.

Key Features:

  • 473 horsepower (G80 M3 model)
  • 6-speed manual transmission
  • Rear-wheel drive for classic handling dynamics
  • Adaptive M suspension for superior road feel
  • 0-60 mph in just 4.1 seconds

If you're looking for a car that prioritizes driving pleasure, the BMW M3 Manual is the perfect choice.
